Support Scope

Elevara support covers AdminLedger for Jira installation, Marketplace licensing, scan behavior, CSV export, Confluence report publishing, privacy, and security questions.

AdminLedger does not automatically modify Jira configuration. Customers remain responsible for reviewing findings before making Jira administration changes.
